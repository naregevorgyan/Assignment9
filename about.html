About Yousuf Karsh

Yousuf Karsh (1908-2002) was a renowned portrait photographer known for his iconic images of famous personalities. From Winston Churchill to Albert Einstein, Karsh's work has been celebrated for its profound ability to reveal the character of his subjects. His photography has left an indelible mark on the world of art and portraiture.
